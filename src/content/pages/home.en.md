## Hi, I'm Marta 👋

I’m naturally curious, always driven by a passion for learning and, above all, a writer who has found in code a new language for storytelling. My background in journalism and creative writing isn’t just a part of my past; it’s the lens through which I view every project —fuelled by an almost academic obsession with clarity, structure, and narrative elegance.

In my view, software is much like literature: it’s born from a human need and should be capable of telling the user's story. This is why, although my technical foundation is solid—built on Java, Kotlin, and Android architectures—I navigate development with the fluidity of someone who understands that technology is a means, not an end. My time living in England taught me two key lessons: the humility that comes with being an outsider, and the ability to lead and train teams in high-intensity, high-demand environments.

These days, I enjoy exploring the versatile nature of programming—from Python to JavaScript— while striving for clean architecture and well-documented code. I’m not just looking to "make it work"; I aim to build robust systems that are, at their core, coherent and maintainable. My projects are the canvas where I apply this unique vision: technical in execution, but deeply human and narrative in spirit.
